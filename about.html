About the Candles

We began creating candles using natural ingredients once we found out how toxic and harmful burning artificial fragrances is. Our candles are made with a blend of soy and coconut wax which is non-toxic in comparison to paraffin wax. We also use essential oils instead of fragrance oils. Essential oils are safer than fragrance oils and throw a softer scent. We use a hemp/cotton wick which we found best for the soy wax. If you have any questions at all do not hesitate to reach out using our form on the contact page. Thank you for your support.
